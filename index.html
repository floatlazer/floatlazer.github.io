Hello, I am Xuan Zhang. I am a final-year PhD student advised by Prof. Shuiwang Ji. I am interested in methods and applications of deep learning. My current research focus is developing deep learning methods for scientific problems, including deep-learning-based physics simulation and computational chemistry.
I received my master's degree in engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, my engineering degree from ENSTA Paris (equivalent to master's degree), and my bachelor's degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
